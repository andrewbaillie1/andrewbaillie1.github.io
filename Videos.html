How I make a video

Due to Non disclousre agreements I can't display any of the videos that I have made here (except Emmi's hair - thanks Emmi!).

However, I can give a short description of the videos I make and how I have made them. CEO Videos: Once a month I makea video of the CEO giving an update to all employees about business performance and other issues and successes that the company is experincing. I work with the CEO as well as his PA to find and write the appropariate content and provide guidelines on what should be covered as well as how to cover it. Office Reopening: One of my more recent video projects was to provide a series of five videos to demonstrate to all 4500 emplyees on the new procedures and how to follow them coming back to the office. This involved engaging with the facilities team to understand the procedures then interpt them into a video script and storyboard before arranging filming in a Covid-19 secure environment.
